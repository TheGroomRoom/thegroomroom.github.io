Our Grooming Process

1

Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your pet's specific needs, any health concerns, and your styling preferences. We assess coat condition, skin health, and temperament.

2

Luxury Bath

Using premium, coat-specific shampoos and warm water, we provide a thorough yet gentle cleansing. Multiple rinses ensure no residue remains.

3

Conditioning Treatment

Application of high-quality conditioner suited to your dog's coat type, followed by a relaxing massage to work the product through.

4

Drying & Brushing

Gentle towel drying followed by professional blow-drying at a comfortable temperature. Thorough brushing to achieve the perfect foundation.

5

Haircut & Styling

Precision cutting using professional techniques. We follow breed standards or your custom style preferences with expert attention to detail.

6

Nail Care & Finishing

Careful nail trimming and filing, ear cleaning, and paw pad care. We finish with a light fragrance spray if desired.

7

Breath Refresh

A quick and gentle dental foam application that freshens your dog’s breath and helps reduce odor-causing bacteria.

8

Final Inspection

A complete quality check to ensure your pet looks and feels their absolute best. We add any finishing touches before your pet is ready to go home.

Our Quality Commitment

Every product we use is carefully selected for its quality, safety, and effectiveness. We continuously research and test new products to ensure we're offering the very best for your pet. All our grooming tools are sanitized between each client, and we maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene.
