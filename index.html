Our Services

Full Grooming

Complete grooming service including a coat-specific shampoo, moisturizing conditioner, blow dry, nail trim and buff, brush and comb, ear cleaning, and breath-refresh

Deshed Services

Deep conditioning treatment before and after shampooing to remove shedding fur, followed by blow out and full body brush and comb

Styling

Professional styling and breed-specific cuts for your beloved pet

Nail Care

Gentle nail trim and buffing and paw pad care

Optional Services

Canine Massage

Promotes relaxation and recovery by easing muscle tension, improving circulation, and supporting overall comfort and body awareness

PEMF Treatment

A gentle, non-invasive treatment that supports the body’s natural healing processes by reducing inflammation, easing chronic discomfort, and improving mobility

Collar Cleaning

Gently deep cleans and refreshes collars by removing built-up dirt, oils, and odors, helping keep your dog’s gear clean, comfortable, and skin-friendly

Creative Grooming

Customized color and artistic enhancements designed to express your dog’s personality using pet-safe products. This service must be booked in advance.
