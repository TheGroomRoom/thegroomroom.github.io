Meet Your Groomer

Get to know the passionate professionals behind every perfect groom. Our team is dedicated to making your pet look and feel their very best.

Mariah

Mariah is the founder of The Groom Room and has been grooming animals professionally for over a decade. Her gentle approach and keen eye for detail have earned her a loyal following of clients and their furry companions. Mariah has a special talent for working with anxious or sensitive dogs, and she stays current with the latest grooming techniques through ongoing education.

Mike

Mike brings warmth, skill, and a calm energy to every appointment. With a background in animal care and a passion for dogs of all breeds and sizes, Mike ensures each pet leaves looking their best and feeling relaxed. He is committed to continuing education and always brings the latest grooming knowledge to The Groom Room.
