Professional Certifications

At The Groom Room, we take pride in our professional training and ongoing education. Our certifications demonstrate our commitment to excellence and ensure that your pet receives the highest quality care from knowledgeable, skilled professionals.

Certified Professional Groomer (CPG)

National Dog Groomers Association of America

Comprehensive certification demonstrating expertise in all aspects of professional dog grooming, including safety protocols, breed-specific styling, and advanced techniques.

Pet First Aid & CPR Certified

Dual Certified

Current certification in pet first aid and CPR, ensuring we can respond appropriately to any emergency situation that may arise.

Canine Behavior & Handling

International Professional Groomers Inc.

Specialized training in understanding canine behavior, stress signals, and proper handling techniques for anxious or difficult-to-groom dogs.

Difficult Dog Handling

Barkleigh

Advanced, behavior-focused program that equips professional groomers with practical techniques to safely, confidently, and compassionately handle challenging dogs in the salon.

Certified Canine Esthetician

Dual Certified

Advanced training in identifying and managing various coat and skin conditions, including appropriate product selection for optimal health.

Safe Grooming Practices

AKC Canine College

Certification in maintaining a safe grooming environment, proper equipment usage, and sanitation protocols to protect both pets and groomers.

Continuing Education

Professional development doesn't stop with certification. We regularly attend grooming seminars, workshops, and conferences to stay current with:

  • Latest grooming techniques and trends
  • New products and tools in the industry
  • Advanced styling methods
  • Animal health and wellness updates
  • Customer service excellence

Our Promise

These certifications and memberships represent our dedication to providing professional, safe, and exceptional grooming services. When you trust us with your beloved pet, you can be confident they are in the hands of trained, certified professionals who genuinely care about their well-being.
